Tests & Progress Tracking

You can't improve what you don't measure. Regular assessments tell you exactly where you stand and what to work on next.

Weekly Speaking Test

Every week, a one-on-one speaking round on a chosen topic. Recorded feedback on fluency, grammar, and pronunciation.

Grammar Test

Practical grammar test every two weeks — tenses, prepositions, articles, and sentence structure in real contexts.

Vocabulary Quiz

Word power assessment with idioms, phrasal verbs, and active vocabulary. Light, fast, and useful.

GD Assessment

Group Discussion graded on opening, contribution, listening, and conclusion. Personalised feedback from the trainer.

Mock Interview

Saturday mock interview rounds. HR-style questions, body language scoring, and answer-structure coaching.

Public Speaking Test

Stage time with a topic, prepare in minutes, speak for a fixed duration. Tests confidence and structure together.

Monthly Progress Report

One-page report at the end of each month — what improved, what to focus on next, and what to practice at home.

Final Assessment

Comprehensive end-of-course assessment covering speaking, GD, interview, public speaking, grammar, and vocabulary.

Certificate of Completion

Awarded on successful completion of the foundation course and final assessment.

How it works

Continuous, low-pressure assessment

Tests at DSEL aren't designed to scare you. They're designed to show you progress. We don't share rankings publicly. Every assessment is followed by personalised feedback so you know exactly what to work on.

  • No public ranking — feedback is one-on-one
  • Speaking-first scoring, grammar second
  • Confidence and clarity weighed equally
  • Retake any test as practice — no penalty

Free placement check

Not sure where to start? Walk in for a 10-minute speaking check. We'll tell you honestly which level fits you best — beginner, intermediate, or advanced practice.
