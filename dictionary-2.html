Dictionary-2 — Verbs

Every verb has 4 forms: V1 (base), V2 (past), V3 (past participle), -ing (present participle). Master these and tenses become easy.

V1 (Base)V2 (Past)V3 (Past Participle)-ing formExample

How to use V1, V2, V3, -ing

  • V1 (base): used in simple present and after modals → "I speak English." / "She can speak."
  • V2 (past): used in simple past → "I spoke to him yesterday."
  • V3 (past participle): used in perfect tenses and passive → "I have spoken." / "It was spoken."
  • -ing form: used in continuous tenses and as a noun → "I am speaking." / "Speaking is a skill."
